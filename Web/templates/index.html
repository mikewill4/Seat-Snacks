Select Venue, Event, and Seat

Select your venue from our list of venues in the venue tab. Then select the event that you are attending and enter your seat information. Now it's time to order from the menu!

  • Select Venue
  • Select Event
  • Enter Section #
  • Enter Row #
  • Enter Seat #
  • Order from the menu

It's as simple as that. We handle the rest.

Order and enjoy!

That's it! Once you place your order, a venue employee will bring your delivery shortly. You can track your current orders to get an estimate of how long you should expect to wait.
